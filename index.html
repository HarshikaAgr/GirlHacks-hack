The Start

Welcome to the Case of the Lost Disco Ball! In this world, you will play the role of a detective in a high-end disco party held in the fanciest dance hall in town. However, the diamond disco ball is missing! The case is to find the disco ball when faced with hidden clues. Please make wise choices, as your choice will determine the fate of the disco ball! However, don't forget to enjoy the music, as it won't last forever! Try your best to fulfill your task while also living in the moment!

“Detective, there’s been a case and we need your help.” You are called by the party organizer and have been asked for help. You are asked to solve the case of the Missing Disco Ball!! You start by looking around the surroundings and see that the dance floor is empty and the diamond disco ball is missing from its place. Suddenly, you hear a commotion in the distance. You look around and see another crowd on the other side of the room. But then, something catches your eye. You see a door that is slightly ajar, with rays of purple light shining through.

Choice B: Head straight towards the commotion
